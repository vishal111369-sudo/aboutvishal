About

Post-sale revenue is my craft

I'm a Customer Success, CRM and retention leader based in Hyderabad. Over 14+ years I've moved from mechanical engineering into B2B technical sales, then product and account management, and now customer lifecycle leadership for B2B SaaS.

My focus is the engine behind recurring revenue: onboarding, activation, retention and reactivation, churn and LTV, segmentation, and the product delivery that supports it. I run programmes that are measured on outcomes — activation, conversion, ARPU/LTV and churn — not vanity volume.

Alongside that, I build. I design and ship AI-assisted tools (see PlayerPulse and my studio, Equibyte) that turn scattered customer data into churn-risk, segmentation and next-best-action for CS teams. It keeps me close to the product and to what modern retention actually needs.

Target: Director / Head of Customer Success at a remote or global B2B SaaS company. I can't relocate from Hyderabad, and I'm open to remote or hybrid with some travel.
